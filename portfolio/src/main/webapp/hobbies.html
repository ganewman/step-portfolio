Ballroom and Latin Dancing

I compete ballroom and latin dancing within the collegiate competitive circuit. I am actually on my college team's board, as the Events Coordinator, which means I am in charge of all space bookings for practices. I have been competing ballroom since I got to college, so for about two years.I just moved up to the silver level this spring. In order, the collegiate levels are roughly as follows:

  1. Newcomer
  2. Bronze
  3. Silver
  4. Gold
  5. Open

There are 19 competitive ballroom dances, falling into four different categories. I don't compete all of them, but I do compete most of them! Here is a table showing the different dances, with links to some examples of my favorites.

There are also many social dances which are not in the main competitive circuit. My three favorites are West Coast Swing, Bachata, and Salsa.

Cross Stitch

Cross stitch is a type of embroidery consisting of many tiny X's as well as the possible addition of specialty stitches such as backstitch (which creates outlines) and french knots (which create the appearance of florets).
