Ballroom and Latin Dancing

I compete ballroom and latin dancing within the collegiate competitive circuit. I am actually on my college team's board, as the Events Coordinator, which means I am in charge of all space bookings for practices. I have been competing ballroom since I got to college, so for about two years.I just moved up to the silver level this spring. In order, the collegiate levels are roughly as follows:

  1. Newcomer
  2. Bronze
  3. Silver
  4. Gold
  5. Open

There are 19 competitive ballroom dances, falling into four different categories. I don't compete all of them, but I do compete most of them! Here is a table showing the different dances, with links to some examples.
